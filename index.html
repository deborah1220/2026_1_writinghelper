🌿 In Bukhansan National Park, we will go plogging along the trail.
This helps the environment because it reduces pollution.
🎁 If you join the plogging tour, you will receive a free local snack box!

북한산 국립공원에서 등산로 플로깅을 할 것입니다. 환경에 도움이 됩니다. 오염을 줄이기 때문입니다.

This helps local people because money stays in the community.
🎁 If you spend over ₩10,000 at local shops, you will receive a 10% discount coupon!

🤝 In Andong Hahoe Village, we will join a traditional mask-making workshop.
This helps local people because it preserves tradition.
🎁 If you bring your own bottle, you will receive a free coffee coupon at the local café!

🎁 개인 텀블러를 가져오면, 현지 카페 무료 커피 쿠폰을 받을 수 있어요!
